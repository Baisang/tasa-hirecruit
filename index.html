About HiRecruit

HiRecruit is a non-profit trade and investment promotion organization in Taiwan with a well-coordinated network of over 600 trained specialists located in more than 40 countries around the world.

About TASA

The Taiwanese American Student Association at the University of California, Berkeley is dedicated to promoting the unity and cooperation of Taiwanese-Americans in the community, the presence of the Taiwanese American community in local and national arenas, and the culture and identity of Taiwanese-Americans.

TASA holds events bringing together men and women of all backgrounds and ideals, uniting them by a common culture and interest, and together building the awareness of Taiwanese-Americans of their own identity and culture, knowledge in their heritage, and strong recognition from others.
